<h1>Dublin's First Multinational Food Tour!</h1>

We are 5 Trinity College Dublin students who love food and want to explore Dulin's multicultural food scene. Join us on our journey as we curate the best food tours for a range of budgets, and cuisines.

We are working on curating walking food tours through Dublin's most vibrant neighbourhoods, connecting people with authentic international cuisines and the stories behind them.

_Created by: Sengul, Bea, Ryan, Zach, Kabir._
